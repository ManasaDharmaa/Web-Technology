Malpe Beach



Malpe Beach is a lovely beach in Udupi district. The sand is white & soft. There is a sea walk built at the end of Malpe Beach from where one can see amazing sunsets. Also there are regular boats to take you to St Mary's Island which is a 15 min ride. There are a number of water sports on the beach. Lovely beach to swim & relax in
